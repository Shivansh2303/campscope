Electrical Engineering is defined as the study, design, and application of equipment, devices, and systems, which use electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. Electrical engineering serves to create innovative electrical systems that enable and enhance modern society while addressing technical and social problems.

Electronics

Electronics engineering is a branch of engineering that deals with the design, development, and application of electronic devices, circuits, and systems. It involves the study of semiconductor materials, transistors, diodes, integrated circuits, and other devices and systems that use electricity.
